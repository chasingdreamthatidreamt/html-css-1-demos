About us

At Shantilly Yoga, we keep our prices down to enable you to experience a unique practice environment in the downtown core. We offer use of mats and props for free. As one of the longest-running yoga studios in Edmonton, we are passionate about what we do. All our teachers have a depth of experience that they bring to each class and workshop. We do not have a one-size-fits-all approach, you will be seen, heard, and valued in our warm and welcoming environment.
